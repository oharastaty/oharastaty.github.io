Welcome to the Chiehstudio Edge FAQ section. Here you will find answers to some of the most common questions about our services.

What is graphic design?

Graphic design is the art of creating visual content to communicate messages. Through applying visual hierarchy and page layout techniques, graphic designers use typography and pictures to meet users' specific needs and focus on the logic of displaying elements in interactive designs.

Why is responsive design important?

Responsive design is crucial because it ensures that a website looks good and functions well on all devices, from desktops to tablets to smartphones. This improves user experience and increases the amount of time visitors stay on your website.

What is the difference between UX and UI?

UX (User Experience) refers to the overall experience a user has while interacting with a product or service, focusing on usability and efficiency. UI (User Interface), on the other hand, involves the visual elements and interactive touchpoints through which a user interacts with a product.

How long does web development take?

The time it takes to develop a website varies based on the project's complexity, features, and functionalities required. On average, a simple website may take a few weeks, while more complex sites with custom features could take several months.

What is user interface design?

User interface design is the process of creating interfaces in software or computerized devices, with a focus on looks or style. It aims to create interfaces that users find easy to use and pleasurable. UI design typically involves the design of graphical user interfaces.
